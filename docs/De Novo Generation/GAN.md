- **GAN-Drug-Generator**: Proposes a framework based on Feedback Generative Adversarial Network (GAN) for the generation and optimization of drug-like molecules, including a multiobjective optimization selection technique.  
    [![Code](https://img.shields.io/github/stars/larngroup/GAN-Drug-Generator?style=for-the-badge&logo=github)](https://github.com/larngroup/GAN-Drug-Generator) 
    [![Last Commit](https://img.shields.io/github/last-commit/larngroup/GAN-Drug-Generator?style=for-the-badge&logo=github)](https://github.com/larngroup/GAN-Drug-Generator) 




- **MoFlowGAN**: MoFlowGAN is a normalizing flow for molecular graphs that is heuristically biased towards easily synthesized, drug-like molecules, aiming to generate high-quality molecular graphs through a process similar to GANs.  
    [![Code](https://img.shields.io/github/stars/thisisntnathan/MoFlowGAN?style=for-the-badge&logo=github)](https://github.com/thisisntnathan/MoFlowGAN) 
    [![Last Commit](https://img.shields.io/github/last-commit/thisisntnathan/MoFlowGAN?style=for-the-badge&logo=github)](https://github.com/thisisntnathan/MoFlowGAN) 

    [![Publication](https://img.shields.io/badge/Publication-Citations:0-blue?style=for-the-badge&logo=bookstack)](https://doi.org/10.26434/chemrxiv-2023-kww) 



- **MolFilterGAN**: MolFilterGAN is a progressively augmented generative adversarial network for triaging AI-designed molecules, focusing on improving the quality of generated molecules by filtering out undesired candidates early in the generation process.  
    [![Code](https://img.shields.io/github/stars/MolFilterGAN/MolFilterGAN?style=for-the-badge&logo=github)](https://github.com/MolFilterGAN/MolFilterGAN) 
    [![Last Commit](https://img.shields.io/github/last-commit/MolFilterGAN/MolFilterGAN?style=for-the-badge&logo=github)](https://github.com/MolFilterGAN/MolFilterGAN) 

    [![Publication](https://img.shields.io/badge/Publication-Citations:10-blue?style=for-the-badge&logo=bookstack)](https://doi.org/10.1186/s13321-023-00711-1) 
    [![JIF](https://img.shields.io/badge/Impact_Factor-7.10-purple?style=for-the-badge&logo=academia)](https://doi.org/10.1186/s13321-023-00711-1)



- **RRCGAN**: RRCGAN combines a generative adversarial network with a regressor to generate small molecules with targeted properties, emphasizing the use of deep learning models to design molecules with specific desired attributes.  
    [![Code](https://img.shields.io/github/stars/linresearchgroup/RRCGAN_Molecules?style=for-the-badge&logo=github)](https://github.com/linresearchgroup/RRCGAN_Molecules) 
    [![Last Commit](https://img.shields.io/github/last-commit/linresearchgroup/RRCGAN_Molecules?style=for-the-badge&logo=github)](https://github.com/linresearchgroup/RRCGAN_Molecules) 




- **SpotGAN**: SpotGAN, a PyTorch implementation of a reverse-transformer GAN, generates scaffold-constrained molecules with property optimization, demonstrating advanced capabilities in generating molecules that adhere to specific structural constraints while optimizing for desired properties.  
    [![Code](https://img.shields.io/github/stars/naruto7283/SpotGAN?style=for-the-badge&logo=github)](https://github.com/naruto7283/SpotGAN) 
    [![Last Commit](https://img.shields.io/github/last-commit/naruto7283/SpotGAN?style=for-the-badge&logo=github)](https://github.com/naruto7283/SpotGAN)
